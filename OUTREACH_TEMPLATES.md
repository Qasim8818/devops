# Outreach Templates

## LinkedIn DM — Version 1 (Direct)

```
Hey [Name] — I build self-healing DevSecOps pipelines. Automated 
monitoring, AI-powered incident response, auto-remediation. I recently 
shipped one from scratch with full observability stack. Would a free 
20-min audit of your current setup be valuable? No sales pitch.
```

---

## LinkedIn DM — Version 2 (Problem-first)

```
Hey [Name] — quick question. Does your team currently get paged at 
3am for incidents that could've been auto-resolved? I built a system 
that eliminates that. Happy to show you how it works — free, no strings.
```

## Email Cold Outreach

**Subject:** Free DevSecOps audit for [Company]

```
Hi [Name],

I came across [Company] and noticed you're scaling your engineering team.

I specialize in self-healing DevSecOps infrastructure — systems that 
monitor, detect, and fix themselves without waking up your engineers.

I'd like to offer a free 20-minute audit of your current pipeline. 
No pitch. Just an honest assessment of where you're exposed.

Worth a quick call?

— Qasim
```

---

## Usage Tips

- **Personalization:** Replace [Name] and [Company] with real research
- **Timing:** Send cold DMs Tuesday-Thursday, 9am-5pm local time
- **Follow-up:** Wait 5 days before follow-up if no response
- **Tracking:** Note which template gets better response rate
- **LinkedIn:** Use 1st connection requests strategically — CTO/VP Eng targets first
